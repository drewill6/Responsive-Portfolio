About Me

Aundrea' Willams (born in the late 1970's), better known as Dre',is happily married and a father of a son who is a junior in college and a daughter who is a senior in high school. Dre' has worked in the medical field in rehab for over thirteen years running a Home Health and Rehabilitation facility. Dre' also has a background in Chemistry which has somehow led him to persue computer science/programming. Dre's biggest interest in programming is developing an application that will allow people to track and maintatin their health and fitness through meal prepping and exercise. Dre' is just one of these health conscious freaks who walks around in spandex pants and shirts. He works out in the stairwell and up and down the hallways at work. Dre' has this vision of reaching out to one million people through word of mouth, without using any form of social media to get themselves healthier. Dre' has been known to ride his bike to work in the morning, which is fifteen miles from his home just because he knew that he was going to miss the gym that day. Dre' is not the tyoe of guy who plays video games or any game for that matter, but said once he becomes a programmer he plans to by a playstation and an xbox and learn to play the most popular game daily for one hour a day until he masters it and learn from it to create his own.
